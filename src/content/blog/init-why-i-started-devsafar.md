people ask me things. what to learn first. what to skip. what comes after this. where to even go.

i am a student myself. online university, not many people to actually talk to. but the ones who find out i build things — they come with questions. and the questions are almost always the same. not "how does this work" but "where do i even start."

that emptiness is what pushed me to start writing.

not a course. not a roadmap. just me writing down what i know, what i am learning, and how i think about it. the stuff i had to figure out alone because there was nothing that spoke to me directly.

the content online is either clickbait, too polished, too old, or written for someone who already has everything figured out. i did not have that. most people i know do not have that either.

i also want to get better at explaining things. i learn fast. explaining is something i am still working on. this blog is practice for that too — i am not going to pretend it is only for other people.

so that is it. no mission statement. i saw a gap, i felt it myself, and i have something to say.

let's go.
